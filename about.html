Meet the planners

We are two lifelong friends who enjoy weddings and helping people so we decided to turn these passions into a business
Simi: Simi is a psychology major with a talent for planning and coordinating weddings. She enjoys using her knowledge of human behavior and interpersonal relationships to create beautiful and meaningful wedding experiences for couples. Simi has planned weddings of various sizes and styles, including intimate backyard ceremonies and large-scale ballroom receptions. She is passionate about using her psychology education to help couples navigate the emotional aspects of wedding planning and make their big day as stress-free as possible. Jessica: Jessica is a creative and organized psychology student who loves planning weddings. She believes that every wedding should be a reflection of the couple's unique personality and love story, and she enjoys collaborating with couples to bring their vision to life. Jessica has experience coordinating various aspects of weddings, including vendor management, budgeting, and timeline creation. She is excited to use her psychology education to better understand and support couples throughout the wedding planning process, and to create unforgettable wedding experiences that celebrate the couple's love.
